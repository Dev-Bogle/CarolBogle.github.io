Hi, I am Carol Bogle. I'm an IT technician with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Information Systems and an aspiring front-end developer and amateur photographer based in St. Martin. My journey into the world of technology began with a fascination for solving complex problems and creating seamless user experiences.

Living on the beautiful island of St. Martin, I am constantly inspired by the vibrant colors and stunning landscapes, which fuel my passion for design and development. I believe that every website should not only function flawlessly but also captivate users with its aesthetic appeal.

With a solid foundation in IT and a keen interest in web development, I am excited to transition from my role as an IT technician to a front-end developer. My background in Computer Information Systems has equipped me with a strong understanding of both technical and business aspects of technology, enabling me to approach web development with a comprehensive perspective. In addition to my professional pursuits, I have a deep love for photography. Capturing the beauty of St. Martin through my lens allows me to blend my technical skills with my creative side, giving me a unique perspective that I bring into my web development projects.

Whether I'm coding a new website, troubleshooting IT issues, or framing the perfect shot, my goal is to create engaging and memorable experiences. I am dedicated to continuous learning and improvement, always striving to enhance my skills and contribute to the ever-evolving field of web development.
